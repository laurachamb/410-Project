Please save the date and plan to join us Nov. 15-17, 2017, at the Curtis Hotel in Denver for CO Impact Days 2017!

Applications for the 400 impact investor spots will open soon. Please sign up on the mailing list to receive notifications at www.coimpactdays.org/signup.

Join visionary investors, innovators, and business leaders who care about Colorado's communities and transforming the model for scaling positive social, environmental, and corporate governance across the state.

CO Impact Days is the first statewide marketplace for impact investing: identifying, connecting, and celebrating top social ventures. CO Impact Days 2016 sourced more than 350 Colorado social ventures, and brought together Colorado’s top 60 social ventures, 200 impact investors — including 92 affiliated with foundations— and 500+ community members in a first of its kind event to Learn. Connect. Invest.

Since the event, at least 20 impact investments have been made, 12 sets of known due diligence are in process, and several foundations are evaluating their endowment for fees, decisions, and impact; and we are helping two private foundations implement best practices in investment governance.

 CO Impact Days 2017 -- Colorado’s proof-point marketplace and the model, tools, and lessons learned -- will not only build on our local success, but simultaneously make the case for scaling this model regionally and nationally to create the National Impact Investing Marketplace.
