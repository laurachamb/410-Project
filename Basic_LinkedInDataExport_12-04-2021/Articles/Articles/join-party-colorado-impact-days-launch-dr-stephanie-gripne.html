 The Colorado Impact Initiative is a 3-year strategy designed to elevate and accelerate impact investing in Colorado, specifically to catalyze the flow of $100 million in investment capital into Colorado-based ventures that deliver positive impact on the state’s communities, economy and natural environment. You are invited to participate in Colorado Impact Days – March 3-4, 2016.  Colorado Impact Initiative and Colorado Impact Day is the first effort to create an impact investing marketplace at a state scale that could be expanded and replicated throughout the nation.

 We are  pleased to announce that the Rose Community Foundation is the official fiscal sponsor of Colorado Impact Initiative and Colorado Impact Days. Additionally, we just received support from the Ford Foundation, The Denver Foundation, Gary Community Investments, LP Brown Foundation, Colorado Trust, Closed Loop Foundation, and Jackson Hole Community Foundation. We are also excited to report that the Colorado Association of Funders and the City of Denver have become formal partners. Finally, we are also continuing to add to our Colorado Day Founder Circle, which includes community leaders and entrepreneurs such as Jim Davidson, Jane Miller, Linda Appel Lipsius, Andrew C. Currie, Rich Hoops, Douglas Smooke, and Jenn Vervier and Corporate Sponsors such as New Belgium Brewery and US Trust.
