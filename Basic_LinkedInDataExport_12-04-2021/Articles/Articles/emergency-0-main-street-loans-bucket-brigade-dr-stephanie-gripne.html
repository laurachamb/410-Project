Impact Finance Center is prototyping the Main Street Bucket Brigade site during the next few days. If it works, we will need the support of the State and business community asap.

1) Let's say you represent a small business such as Muggs Coffee Shop, startup such as Community Funded, or nonprofit such as Impact Finance Center. Each Company or Nonprofit can submit their story and they will be able to create a campaign on this site and invite anyone to do three things: 1) pre-sale purchase coffee 2) donate to Muggs Coffee Shop, or 3) lend money to the Muggs Coffee Shop through 0% Main Street Loan Program (they will have to leave the site to do it) to a partner such as Colorado Lending Source. They will be able to invite friends and family to the Main Street Marketplace to support them and others. One-stop shopping to pre-purchase, donate, or lend money until other government resources can help out.

2) Once we test and launch the system, we need the State to host several virtual convenings with associations of individuals, foundations, donor advised funds, foundations, and family offices to teach them about the Enterprise Integrated Full Spectrum Capital Investment and raise $100M to lend out at 0%. That's $10M in grants + $10M @ 2% + $80M at 6% [the entire loan will be guaranteed up to 25% loss]. We have an oral legal opinion that this is a legal transaction and need to build a campaign around it. See graphic below.

3) Lastly, we at Impact Finance Center, need to finish our fundraising - we are raising $400,000K to match our $100,000K that The Anschutz Foundation just provided us, in order to focus on the Main Street Bucket Brigade.
